Canterbury Local History Association

The Canterbury Local History Association is an organisation that specialises in the local history of Canterbury, New Zealand (Waitaha, Aotearoa). On our website we have information on interesting places in Canterbury that have historical significance. We are a group of volunteers who are passionate about preserving and sharing the rich history of Canterbury.
